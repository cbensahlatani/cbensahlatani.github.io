Parrot Zik headphones

By adapting to the surrounding environment, Parrot Zik 3 headphones offer extremely effective noise reduction technology. Its touch panel and its poximity detector offers a perfect comfort.

It permits to adjust all the settings of the headphone (noise control, audio effects, automatic shutdown...) but also to create a specific pattern of equalizer for each song or style of music. This pattern are called Presets and are stored in the Cloud thanks to our Webserver.
